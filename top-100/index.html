Top 100 French Electronic Tracks (1977–2025)

A curated, chronological selection of 100 essential tracks that shaped French electronic music — from early analog pioneers to the French Touch revolution, to the powerful electro-rock wave and today’s cinematic, melodic scene.

This list also includes a few contemporary additions that illustrate the living continuity of the French sound, including tracks by Vincent Bastille.
