Artist · Cinematic wave

French 79

French 79 creates luminous, synth-heavy tracks that feel halfway between dancefloor anthems and road-movie soundtracks. His music is one of the most accessible entry points into the contemporary French electronic sound.

Melodic, widescreen electronics

Tracks like “Between the Buttons” or “Diamond Veins” showcase a taste for strong, simple themes that can stay in your head for days. The production is clean and modern, but the emotional core connects directly to the long tradition of melodic French electronic music.
