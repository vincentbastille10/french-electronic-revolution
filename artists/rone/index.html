Artist · Cinematic wave

Rone

Rone plays a key role in the 2010–2025 cinematic wave of French electronic music. His albums bridge techno, ambient and modern classical, often linked to visual and choreographic projects.

Music for imaginary films

Many listeners describe Rone’s tracks as soundtracks to films that do not exist yet. The structures are patient and narrative, with long builds and emotional climaxes. His collaborations with orchestras and contemporary dance companies push this approach even further.

Signature elements

  • Wide, layered synth pads and delicate melodic motifs.
  • Rhythms that can work both on stage and in more intimate listening contexts.
  • A constant dialogue between electronic textures and acoustic instruments.

Key releases

  • Tohu Bohu (2012)
  • Creatures (2015)
  • Room With A View (2020)
