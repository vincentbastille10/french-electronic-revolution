Artist · Cinematic wave

The Blaze

The Blaze are as much filmmakers as they are producers. Their tracks move slowly, building intensity around vocals and chords, while their videos tell stories about identity, masculinity and community.

Between club and cinema

Musically, The Blaze sit in a unique space: their beats are simple and steady, almost ritualistic, leaving room for emotional vocals and evolving harmonies. Visually, each release arrives with a strong, self-contained film, making the duo one of the most recognisable acts of the 2010s French scene.

Key videos and tracks

  • Virile
  • Territory
  • Heaven

The Blaze are central to the cinematic wave described in this guide, where visuals and sound are conceived as one object.
